About Us

Read a Book Challenge is a one of a kind challenge for all bookworms. The challenge is to simply read a few books until they're picked back up in a month. Once you accept it expect a beautiful handpicked parcel of books delivered to your doorstep.

We are an initiative created with the aim of rekindling the spirit of reading among today’s Bangaloreans – a trend which seems to be on the dwindle, with the advent of technology and the lack of time furnished by today’s lifestyle. 

What began as a symbiosis of a few friends trying to achieve their common goal of making more people read, Read a Book Challenge is now an attempt at bridging the gap between those who have a lot of books lying around unused and those who want to constantly explore the world of words.
