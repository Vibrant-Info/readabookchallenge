FAQ

 

GENERAL

1. What is the Challenge?
Read is a Book Challenge is an online initiative to encourage the love for reading. The challenge is to simply read a couple a books in a month. These books are selected by our algorithm based on your interests, wish list and books you’ve read in the past and we also facilitate the delivery and pickup of these books.

2. How do I cancel the challenge?
Currently the challenge is applicable for a month. In case you have books with you, select return books and cancel/pause the challenge for the month. If you want to take up the challenge for another month simply press the start challenge button!

3. What is my Library?
“My Library” is your own personal virtual library which you can populate by adding all the books you’ve read in the past in your entire life! Our algorithm makes sure we don’t send you books that you’ve already read in the past that are in your library.

4. What is my Wish list?
“My Wishlist” is the list of all books you wish to read. While selecting books for you our algorithm will give first preference to the books in your wish list! We have integrated with Google Books API so you can add as many books as you want in your wish list, the more the better!

5. What’s the ‘currently reading’ tab?
Currently reading tab shows you the books that have been delivered to you from our side and currently in your possession that you’re reading. Once you return the books the books from this tab will shift to your library automatically.

6. Is this service available outside Bangalore?
We only operate in Bangalore, India at the moment but will soon be launching operations in other cities.

7. How many days will I get to complete the books?
The challenge is applicable for 1 month, but you have the option of taking an extension of up to 1 month just in case you haven’t finished reading those books.

8. Can we request? Search books Author wise/ How do i look for Author wise classification of books?
You cannot see our catalogue and request books but you can add them to your wishlist and we try our best to give books from your wish list.

9. I’m a bloger, bookstagram account owner, vlogger and I want a package to review !
Sure! Please mail us at readabookchallenge@gmail.com and wel get in touch with you regarding it!

Step 1 - Click on Accept Challenge icon on the website;
Step 2 - Complete all personal details (Delivery Address, Contact Information etc.),
Step 3 - Pay an online refundable deposit and have the package delivered.
(The challenge timer on your profile will start ticking right after you have received the package of books. Also, make sure you build your complete reader profile for a better experience.)

3. Do I have to return the books I receive?
Yes Of course. This inventory is owned by our Endorsers and must be returned to us in ‘as was’ condition. We are currently offering books only on Rent.

4. How do I extend the time if I haven't completed the Challenge?
If you haven’t completed reading your books for the month click on Extend Icon on your profile and get an extension of 1-4 weeks, chargers will be applicable accordingly.

Note – if you don’t request a pick up or extend your package, we’ll call you and attempt a default pick up at the delivery address.

5. Can I choose/request a particular book/set of books?
There is no catalogue that you can pick from, but you can enter the specific books in your wish list and we’ll try to send the books if we do have it with us.

6. Does this system work on a subscription basis?
Currently we don’t have a subscription basis; it’s a simple pay as you go model. Register for your package on a monthly basis to receive the package.

7. How does the Deposit system work?
We charge a deposit as security for the books that we are lending to you. In case of loss or damages to the books, charges to the deposit will be applicable. A standard deposit of Rs 500 for any number of books is applicable. You need to have paid the deposit to subscribe for the challenge and receive books. This is a refundable deposit with a transfer charge of 4% applicable, that may be withdrawn after the books have been returned.

6. Where will my deposit be refunded ?
The deposit will be refunded in the Paytm number that you provide at the time of requesting fro a refund. Please note that a transfer charge of 4% is applicable.

7. What is the minimum I have to deposit?
500 is the minimum deposit that is required at any given point in time to take up the challenge.

8. How does the deposit withdrawal work?
You will be required to make a withdrawal request, give us your Paytm account details and expect to receive the amount in 7-10 days (that gives us enough time to check on any dues that you might have).

9. What happens when I run out of the 30 day reading time?
After 30 days you may make a pick up request if you’ve finished reading the books and order a new package in place of it or you may take an extension of 1-4 weeks and pay the Extention accordingly.

10. What is the paytm account for?
The Paytm account is required to only withdraw the deposit amount that has been paid.

11. Why is it important to build my profile?
It is important for you to complete all details of your profile so that you don’t receive books that you’ve already read and instead receive books that you like and wish to read. Our first priority will be to make sure you receive books from your wish list, or at the least give you books that are in preference of your genre.

12. What happens if I loose/damage a book?
Based on the severity of damages the charges will be applicable to the deposit amount. In case of loss of book, the price of the book will have to be paid in full.

ENDORSERS

1. Who is an endorser?
An endorser is a vendor/owner of books who willingly contributes his books to our inventory.

2. How do I become an endorser?
You can become an endorser if you have books lying around at home and wish to make some money off of it, fill out the endorsee form and we will get back to you.

DELIVERY PICKUP

1. What is your return policy?
The books have to be returned at the end of the month or after the extension period. In case a book is damaged or is not returned we hold the right to withdraw the necessary amount from your deposit.

2. How do I request for return of book?
Click on “return books” and select if you want a new package or not and we will schedule a pickup from our end.

3. Do I have to pay once the books are delivered or returned?
The payment has to be done on delivery and the payment includes charges for delivery, pickup, packaging and the books itself! There is no extra charge on pickup.

4. How much time will the books take to reach me/what is the delivery timeline?
We will take up to 6 working days to deliver the books once a request is placed since we need to select, assign and do a personalised packing of the books. In case of COD a call will be made before attempting a delivery to you.

5. What are the delivery charges?
Delivery/Pickup charges of Rs 25 is applicable for up to 3 books only. For any order of more than 3 books the delivery and pickup is absolutely free.

6. Can I track my delivery?
No. We don’t have an option of delivery tracking from the customers end yet. But you will be informed via SMS on every stage of delivery.

7. How do I change shipping/contact details?
Click on the arrow on the profile and change the shipping details. Alternatively you can also change shipping details at the time of checkout.

8. What are the terms of delivery?
A delivery attempt is made twice to the customer, after which the books are returned to our office. A COD delivery is only attempted after calling the customer at the time of delivery. We prefer online payment as it is more convenient for the delivery boys and even for the customer to collect books through a neighbour in case the customer is not available at home if payment is already done online.

9. What if I’m unavailable at the time of delivery/pickup ?
You can leave the books with your neighbour/watchman in advance at the time of delivery and pickup of books. We will inform you in advance if a delivery attempt is being on that day of your convenience. We prefer online payment in such a case as it becomes easy for the delivery boy to deliver books if payment is already done online.
