How it Works

 

How it Works

Read A Book Challenge is a platform which intends to bring together those people who have books which they have already read and have lying around the house – by monetising the same – and those who want to delve into titles and genres they never have. So simply put, the initiative takes the books from the “Haves” and lends it to the “Want to Read”s for a month.

For Readers

Once you register, all you need to do is await your monthly package. Every package consists of a few books – which are a surprise – and all you need to do, is read them. This package will be delivered to your address on the 1st or 15th of each month and at the end of 30 days, you need to return the books and get a new package for the next month. In case you are not able to finish reading those books,you have the option of extending your package for 2 weeks or for another month, for a nominal charge. 

Shouldn’t there be more to? Nope… We’re afraid not!

For Endorsers

Ever wondered what to do with all the books you have lying around your house – those that you’ve already finished reading? Well, Read a Book Challenge is the perfect opportunity for such people, who can endorse their books to us and have them put to use by allowing multiple other readers have access to them. What’s more, you get paid a sum for each book and can call for the book, whenever you want it back… Give yourself a little pinch – nope, you are not dreaming.
